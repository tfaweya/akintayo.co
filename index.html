About

Akintayo Adebiyi is a visionary entrepreneur whose work spans the fields of real estate and agribusiness. With an MSc in Innovation Management and Entrepreneurship from the University of Manchester, he has built a career focused on bridging market gaps, optimizing value chains, and driving sustainable growth. His expertise extends across both public and private sectors, where he has led high-impact projects that improve access to essential goods, streamline logistics, and create economic opportunities.

Transforming Agribusiness and Real Estate

Early Years: Building Foundations in Policy and Agribusiness

Akintayo Adebiyi’s journey into entrepreneurship and economic development began with his tenure at the Office of the Governor, State of Osun, where he played a key role in policy-making and economic strategy. From 2016 to 2019, he served as a Programme Analyst, tracking MSME growth and wealth creation initiatives. His keen analytical skills and dedication to economic development led to a promotion as Unit Head in 2018, where he spearheaded major projects that strengthened both agricultural and non-agricultural value chains.

One of his most impactful contributions was the creation of a nationwide distribution network supporting over 5,000 agribusinesses, providing them with market access and sustainable growth opportunities. Additionally, he championed community-based training programs, equipping farmers and small business owners with the knowledge and tools to expand their enterprises.

Following his time in government, Akintayo transitioned into the private sector, taking on a leadership role at Freshfield Produce Aggregation Limited from 2019 to 2021. Here, he led the Aje-Foodmart Initiative, which optimized the aggregation, logistics, storage, and distribution of farm produce from Osun to Lagos, improving food supply chain efficiency. He also pioneered Aje-Mart, a digital marketplace for MSMEs, helping local businesses gain visibility through branding and training programs, and positioning them for national and international markets.

Currently: Leading Innovation in Agribusiness and Real Estate

Today, Akintayo Adebiyi is at the helm of two major ventures—Creel Africa and Essi Hospitality Development— where he continues to drive innovation in agribusiness and real estate.

As the Founder & CEO of Creel Africa, he is focused on sustainable food production and bridging supply gaps in agriculture. His company specializes in the production and distribution of catfish, snails, cassava, and maize, ensuring high-quality, locally sourced products reach both domestic and international markets. Through strategic planning and responsible agricultural practices, he has positioned Creel Africa as a key player in Nigeria’s agribusiness sector.

Beyond agriculture, Akintayo has made significant strides in the real estate and hospitality industry as Executive Director at Essi Hospitality Development. Under his leadership, the company has expanded its short-let property portfolio in Lagos and Ibadan, driving increased occupancy and profitability. His expertise in market analysis and innovative service strategies has strengthened the brand’s positioning, leading to substantial revenue growth.

Additionally, he has overseen large-scale construction projects, managing teams of architects, project managers, and engineers to deliver high-quality developments on time and within budget. His ability to balance operational efficiency with strategic expansion has made him a force in Nigeria’s real estate landscape.

From policy-making to agribusiness and real estate development, Akintayo Adebiyi’s career has been defined by innovation, impact, and economic empowerment. As he continues to shape industries and create opportunities, his work stands as a testament to his vision for sustainable growth in Nigeria and beyond.

About Us
Creel Africa is an innovative agricultural company dedicated to delivering high-quality, sustainably produced agricultural products directly to consumers and businesses. Founded in 2020, we have quickly established ourselves as a trusted industry leader, specializing in the production, sale, and distribution of premium African proteins like catfish and snails, as well as essential crops such as cassava and maize.

Our Mission
With our origins in Nigeria, our goal is to address supply gaps in the agricultural sector and meet the growing demand for fresh, nutritious produce. Our commitment to sustainability guides all our operations, ensuring a consistent and reliable supply chain that serves both local markets and the global African diaspora.
